The Bestest Burger!

Description

This is the bestest burger you can make at home! No more dry, lackluster hamburgers. These are juicy, and spices can be easily added or changed to suit anyone's taste. If you find the meat mixture too mushy, just add more bread crumbs until it forms patties that hold their shape.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 3/4 cup dry bread crumbs
  • 3 tablespoons evaporated milk
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauces
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Steps

    Step 1

  1. Gather all Ingredients

    2. Step 2

  2. Preheat a grill for high heat

    3. Step 3

  3. Mix the ground beef, egg, breat cumbs, evaporated milk, Worcestershire sauce, cayenne pepper, and garlic in a large bowl using your hands

    4. Step 4

  4. Form the mixture into 8 hamburger patties

    5. Step 5

  5. lightly oil the grill grate. Grill patties until browned and no longer pink, about 5 minutes per side.

    6. Step 6

  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
